About Elon Musk

Elon Musk, born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa, is a technology entrepreneur, engineer, and investor known for founding and leading multiple groundbreaking companies. He is the CEO and lead designer of SpaceX, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc., and founder of The Boring Company, Neuralink, and X (formerly Twitter).

Musk’s career has been defined by his relentless pursuit of innovation. At SpaceX, he has advanced the development of reusable rockets and is actively working toward the colonization of Mars. At Tesla, he revolutionized the electric vehicle industry, accelerating the global shift toward sustainable energy.

Beyond his corporate achievements, Musk’s influence extends into artificial intelligence, high-speed transportation through Hyperloop, and satellite internet connectivity via Starlink. His vision consistently merges technology and sustainability to address some of humanity’s greatest challenges.
